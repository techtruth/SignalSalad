# Vision

## Why This Exists

SignalSalad exists to provide a real, working, production-grade reference for realtime signaling and WebRTC media orchestration.

The goal is a practical, logical, media signaling service that anyone can run, learn from, and build on.

## Open and Free Commitment

This system will remain open and free to use.

- No hidden feature walls
- No proprietary lock-in traps
- No intentional "gotcha" split between demo and real capability

The core system in this repository is intended to stay fully usable as a complete base platform.

## Big Goals

- Advance a clean and understandable reference architecture for scaling WebRTC systems
- Prove reliability through transparent tests and diagnostics
- Support teams who need a trustworthy baseline for medium-to-large WebRTC projects 

## Revenue Opportunity

There is money to be made by helping teams:

- customize SignalSalad for their product and workflows
- integrate deeper infrastructure and deployment patterns
- add organization-specific requirements (security, scaling, observability, compliance)
- operate and harden deployments in real environments
- set up this codebase directly in your infrastructure
- build working, company-specific demos and pilot environments
- provide general architecture and implementation consulting around realtime systems

Teams need the ability to setup and evaluate the complete project for free.
There should be no benefit to complicate, obfuscate, or hinder the core project.

In short:
 - The full and complete system stays open and free 
 - Paid work is in tailoring, integrating, and operation
